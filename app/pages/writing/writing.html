Comedy Writing

In addition to her background in legal and academic writing, Marsden studied creative non-fiction writing at UNC Chapel Hill, humorous memoir at Second City, and TV writing at iO’s Comedy Lab. She has shared her humorous non-fiction stories at the Monte in North Carolina, as well as You’re Being Ridiculous at the Steppenwolf Theatre, We Have Questions at the De Maat Studio Theatre, and multiple showcases at Second City’s Judy’s Beat Lounge. She’s also on the ensemble of the Personal Disclosures Podcast, where she shares and discusses original humorous non-fiction stories.

Marsden was head-writer for iO’s February 2018 Monday Night Live Showcase and was involved in writing and developing sketches for Funemployed, a sketch show which ran in Second City’s Blackout Cabaret.
