About Marsden

Marsden Kelly is a Chicago-based writer & comedian. Best known as the ‘Ruth Bader Ginsburg of Comedy,’ Marsden has been shamelessly serving Seasonal Affective Disorder REALNESS since 2012. Her work pushes people to ask the big questions, like, “but, why?” “seriously?” and “How are you still alive?” Check out her homepage for information on all of her upcoming projects and performances.
